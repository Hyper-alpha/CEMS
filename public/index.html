About CEMS

The College Event Management System (CEMS) is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline event management for educational institutions. Our system provides a seamless experience for students, organizers, and administrators.

For Students

Discover and register for events, track your participation, and earn certificates.

For Organizers

Create and manage events, track attendance, and analyze feedback.

For Admins

Oversee all events, manage users, and generate comprehensive reports.
